Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

Appzgarden Studio ("we," "our," or "us") operates the B&W Photo/Video mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Key Point: We respect your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data remains on your device.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Our App is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Any information about your photos or videos

2. How the App Works

The B&W Photo/Video app operates entirely on your device:

  • Camera Access: Used only to capture photos and videos directly on your device
  • Storage Access: Used only to save converted black and white media to your device's local storage
  • Media Library Access: Used only to allow you to select existing photos and videos from your device for conversion
  • Microphone Access: Used only when recording videos with audio

3. Data Storage

All data generated by the App is stored locally on your device:

  • Converted photos and videos are saved in the app's private storage directory
  • Media metadata (titles, timestamps, favorites) is stored in a local database on your device
  • No data is uploaded to any server or cloud service
  • You have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time

4. Permissions Required

The App requires the following permissions to function:

Camera Permission

Required to capture new photos and videos. This permission is only used when you actively choose to take a photo or record a video.

Storage/Media Permission

Required to:

  • Save converted black and white photos and videos
  • Access your existing photos and videos for conversion
  • Share media with other apps

Microphone Permission

Required only when recording videos with audio. Not used for any other purpose.

5. Third-Party Services

Our App does NOT use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud storage services

6. Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data. When you use the share functionality within the App:

  • Media is shared directly from your device to the app you choose
  • We do not intercept, store, or transmit the shared content
  • Sharing is handled by your device's operating system

7. Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App can be safely used by users of all ages as it operates entirely offline and does not collect personal information.

8. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security measures (lock screen, encryption, etc.)
  • We recommend keeping your device secure with a strong password or biometric authentication
  • Regularly back up your device if you want to preserve your converted media

9. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally and accessible through the App
  • Delete: You can delete individual media items or clear all data through the App
  • Export: You can share or export your media to other apps or services at any time
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the App will remove all associated data from your device

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

If we make material changes that affect how we handle data (though we currently don't collect any), we will notify you through the App or other appropriate means.

11. International Users

Since our App does not collect or transmit any data, there are no international data transfer concerns. The App works the same way regardless of your location, with all processing happening locally on your device.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

13. European Users (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since we do not collect, process, or store any personal data, GDPR data processing requirements do not apply to our App's core functionality.
