Privacy Policy — GeoCamera

1. Introduction

GeoCamera ("the App") is developed and maintained by AppzGarden Studio ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use GeoCamera on your Android device.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

GeoCamera does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data remains on your device.

The App accesses the following solely for functionality:

  • Location (GPS) — Used to capture coordinates, address, altitude, and accuracy at the moment of capture. No background tracking.
  • Camera — Capture photos and videos.
  • Microphone — Record audio during video capture.
  • Storage / Media — Read and save photos/videos on your device.

3. How We Use This Information

  • Stamp geo information (location, coordinates, time, etc.) on media
  • Display photos/videos in app library and map
  • Enable sharing via device sharing options

No data is sent to us or any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

Google Maps: Used for map display. Google may collect anonymized usage data.

OpenStreetMap: Used for map tiles. Requests include coordinates only.

5. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers or cloud storage. Uninstalling the app removes app settings. Media remains until deleted manually.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share data. Media is only shared when you explicitly use the share feature.

7. Children's Privacy

GeoCamera is not intended for children under 13.

8. Permissions Summary

Permission Purpose
ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION Capture precise GPS location
ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION Fallback location
CAMERA Capture photos/videos
RECORD_AUDIO Record video audio
READ_MEDIA_IMAGES Import images
READ_MEDIA_VIDEO Import videos
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Save media (Android 9 and below)
INTERNET Load maps and tiles

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the App means you accept the changes.
